Our Locations

Convenient tennis coaching venues across eastern Singapore

Training Locations Overview

Tennis Learning in Singapore offers coaching at various locations throughout the eastern region, making it convenient for residents to find a court near them. All our locations are carefully selected to provide quality playing surfaces and appropriate facilities for effective training.

Public Court Booking Information

Public courts require separate booking and payment through the ActiveSG portal. Coach Jack will guide you through the booking process if needed. Court fees typically range from $3-$8 per hour for Singapore citizens/PRs and $12-$15 for non-residents.

ActiveSG Booking Portal

Private Condominiums

For residents of private condominiums in eastern Singapore, we offer the convenience of tennis coaching at your own residential facilities. This option eliminates the need for travel and court booking.

Simei Area

Coaching available at various condominiums including Tropical Spring, Eastpoint Green, and The Palette.

Tampines Area

Coaching available at various condominiums including Q Bay Residences, The Santorini, and Tampines Court.

Pasir Ris Area

Coaching available at various condominiums including Ripple Bay, Coco Palms, and Livia.

Other East Areas

Coaching also available at select condominiums in Bedok, Changi, and Loyang areas upon request.

Convenience

Group Options

Private Condominium Requirements

For coaching at private condominiums, you must be a resident or have guest access to the facilities. Coach Jack will need to be registered as a visitor according to your condominium's security protocols. Please check your condominium's rules regarding external coaches before booking.

Booking Information

When booking your tennis coaching session, please keep the following in mind:

  • Location Selection: Choose a location that's convenient for you from our available options
  • Court Availability: Public courts may require separate booking through ActiveSG
  • Back-to-Back Sessions: Schedule consecutive sessions at the same location to avoid travel time
  • Weather Considerations: All locations are outdoor courts and subject to weather conditions
  • Special Requests: If you have a preferred location not listed, please contact us to discuss options
