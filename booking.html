Booking FAQs

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 3-5 days in advance to secure your preferred time slot, especially for peak hours and weekends. Last-minute bookings are possible but subject to availability.

How do I pay for my session?

After your booking is confirmed, you'll receive payment instructions via email. We accept bank transfers, PayNow, and major credit cards. Payment must be completed at least 3 hours before your session.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made at least 24 hours before the scheduled session qualify for a full refund or rescheduling. Late cancellations may incur a fee, except in cases of inclement weather or emergencies.

Can I reschedule my session?

Yes, you can reschedule through the link in your confirmation email. Rescheduling requests should be made at least 24 hours before your original session time to avoid any fees.

What happens if it rains?

If a session is stopped due to rain, the remaining minutes will be added to a future training session. We'll contact you to arrange a convenient time to complete your session.

How do I book for multiple people?

For group sessions, select "Group Session" as your session type and indicate the number of participants in the notes section. All participants should be of similar skill levels for the most effective coaching.
