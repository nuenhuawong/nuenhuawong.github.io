Tennis Coaching Opportunity for Enthusiastic Students In Singapore

⚡ Let's collaborate to reach and exceed your tennis goals together ⚡

About the Coach

  • Experienced and certified tennis coach
  • Tailored coaching sessions for players of levels from beginners to intermediate
  • Strongly believes in progressive training and personalized corrections
  • Facilitates effective interaction in both Mandarin and English

What You Can Expect

  • Personalized coaching tailored to your strengths and areas for improvement
  • Focused training to enhance your techniques, footwork, and game strategy
  • Advice on off-court exercises to boost performance
  • User-friendly booking system for easy scheduling
  • Confirmation and reminder emails for your booking will be sent 24 hours and 4 hours prior to the scheduled start time
  • You can reschedule or cancel your booking directly through the link provided in your confirmation email
  • If a tennis session is stopped due to rain, the remaining minutes will be added to a future training session
  • The coach reserves the right to cancel a confirmed booking if payment is not received at least 3 hours before the session starts

  • Tennis Aid

Who Can Join

  • All age groups are welcome!
  • Group and individual sessions are available
  • Open to residents in the East of Singapore
    1. Public Court: Kallang Tennis Centre, Bedok Heartbeat, Tampines Sport Hub, and Pasir Ris Tennis Centre
    2. Private Condo: Simei, Tampines, and Pasir Ris (and others in the east of Singapore)

Peak Hour

  • Weekdays from 6:00 PM to 10:00 PM
  • Weekends and public holidays from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM

Steps to Get Started

    1. Check coach availability
      • When selecting your preferred time slot, make sure back-to-back sessions are scheduled at the same location. Avoid booking consecutive slots at different locations to account for coach travel time.
    2. Book your court (To be completed by the student)
    3. Book coach time slot
      • Each booking is for one time slot only, and the duration of each time slot is one hour.
      • Once the booking is completed, the coach will approve it after verification.
    4. Kindly make the payment (PayNow is preferred)

Disclaimer

I reserve the right to cancel bookings at any time, should the need arise. Thank you for your understanding
